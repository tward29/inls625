Interests

I am involved in many activities outside of school. The following are my main interests, as well as a list of 10 books that I would bring with me to a deserted island:

Sports

I grew up playing many different sports. I still love to watch sports, but my favorite is baseball. Baseball was my main sport for the majority of my life and remain involved with it. I am currently on the UNC Baseball Analytics Team, so I am able to combine my love of baseball with my analytical skills.

Accounting

I am particularly interested in pursuing a career in accounting. I have analytical skills and feel that the accounting career path will provide me with many opportunities. The Undergraduate Accounting Club has been a great way for me to get involved and learn more about the industry. It has helped me to meet many of the employers and I am currently in the process of applying for internships. The club has given me a great platform to network with professionals.
